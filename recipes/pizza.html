Italian Cultural Food

Bake your pizza dough with sauce, cheese, and toppings until golden and bubbly. Slice and serve hot, optionally drizzling with olive oil or adding fresh herbs on top.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Prepare dough: Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness
  2. Add sauce: Spread tomato or pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust
  3. Add cheese & toppings: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese, then add fresh basil
  4. Bake: Place pizza in the oven for 12â€“18 minutes, until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly
